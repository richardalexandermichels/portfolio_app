My Story

I grew up in Glendale Arizona, majored in film studies at Arizona State University and now I'm a software developer living in Brooklyn. My dad got me into computer and basic web technology at a young age, teaching HTML and CSS. Enough to make a simple homepage with bookmarks to my favorite sites.

In college I found myself saddled with a new found passion for film and ended up with a degree in Film and Media Studies. However, life has a crazy way of getting you to suddenly move to Brooklyn. It was here that my obsession with technology and web development was refreshed. I studied using a wide variety of online resources (Khan Academy, Coursera, Udacity etc.) and eventually landed a spot at Fullstack academy of code.

After Fullstack I quickly got my first professional software development job where I stayed for over 3 years. It was at a very small company so I was imedieatly given some subtantial responsibiities. I was able to learn Java, SQL, and Spring well enough to eventually become the lead software developer there. However, as much as I learned I knew I had reached a plateau and am currently on the look out for a new company where I can further my career.
