My Story

I grew up in Glendale Arizona, majored in Film at Arizona State University and now I'm a software developer living in Brooklyn. My dad got me into computer technology and coding at a young age, teaching me basic HTML and CSS. Enough to make a simple homepage with bookmarks to my favorite sites, you know, various sparkling GeoCities pages, HOTBOT search engine and my Juno email. In college I found myself saddled with a new found passion for film and ended up with a degree in Film and Media. However, life has a crazy way of getting you to suddenly move to Brooklyn. It was here that my obsession with technology and web development took hold. I studied the basics using the wide variety of online resources (Khan Academy, Coursera, Udacity etc.) and eventually landed a spot at Fullstack academy of code (around a 5% acceptance rate at the time I got in FYI). After Fullstack I quickly got my first professional software development job where I stayed for over two years. It was at a very small company so I was imedieatly given some subtantial responsibiities. I taught myself Java, SQL, and Spring well enough to eventually become the lead software developer there. However, as much as I learned I knew I had reached a plateau and am currently on the look out for a new company where I can grow substantially as a developer.
